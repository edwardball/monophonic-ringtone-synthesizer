Monophonic Ringtone Synthesizer

You can play the Monophonic Ringtone Synthesizer in three ways.

First, you can press the keys on your Qwerty keyboard. For example A, W, S, E play the notes C, C#, D, D# and so on.

Secondly, you can click the notes with your mouse (or tap with your finger).

Thirdly, if you are using Chrome, you can connect a MIDI keyboard, and play via that method. If it doesn't work first time, try refreshing or re-connecting.

If using the Qwerty Keyboard method, here are some example tunes to get you started.

  • Nokia Theme (Gran Vals)
    ;lty ojsd jhwdh
  • Tubular Bells
    dhdjdghdkdldjk dhdjdghdkdldjkdjd
  • The Entertainer
    sedk d k d k klp;kl;jlk
  • Happy Birthday
    aasafd aasagf aakh ffds uuhfgf
